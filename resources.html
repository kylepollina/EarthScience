NOAA

Datasets officially released by the United States National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)

  • NOAA Climate Data Online - Online climate datasets
  • Mauna Loa CO2 Observatory - Trends in Atmospheric Carbon Dioxide from Mauna Loa, Hawaii
  • Deep Sea Corals - Coral Records from NOAA’s Deep-Sea Coral Research and Technology Program
  • Severe Weather Data Inventory - An integrated database of severe weather records for the United States
  • Global Surface Summary of the Day - Daily global surface summary from over 9000 weather stations from 1929 to now
    • This is a BigQuery dataset. This dataset is also available for download (3GB)
  • Daily Global Historical Climatology Network - NOAA’s Global Historical Climatology Network (GHCN) is an integrated database of climate summaries from land surface stations across the globe that have been subjected to a common suite of quality assurance reviews
    • This is a BigQuery dataset. This dataset is also available for download (14GB)
  • Precipitation 15 Minute - This dataset contains 15-minute precipitation data (reported 4 times per hour, if precip occurs) for U.S. stations along with selected non-U.S. stations in U.S. territories and associated nations
  • ICOADS - A global marine meteorological and surface ocean dataset
  • Hurricanes and Typhoons, 1851-2014 - Location, wind, and pressure of tropical cyclones in Atlantic and Pacific Oceans
  • Tsunami Causes and Waves - Cause, magnitude, and intensity of every tsunami since 2000 BC
  • NOAA GOES-16 - Next generation geostationary weather satellites data
  • NOAA U.S. Climatic Normals - Climate Normals are three-decade averages of climatological variables including temperature and precipitation

EPA

Datasets officially released by the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

  • Historical Air Quality - Air Quality Data Collected at Outdoor Monitors Across the US
  • Air Quality Annual Summary - A summary of air quality from 1987 to 2017
  • Carbon Monoxide - A summary of daily CO levels from 1990 to 2017
  • Vehicle Fuel Economy Estimates, 1984-2017 - Which makes and models have the highest city and highway MPG?
  • Toxic Release Inventory - US EPA data on release of toxic chemicals for 1987-2016
  • Hazardous Air Pollutants - A summary of daily Hazardous Air Pollutants from 1990 to 2017
  • Vehicle Fuel Economy - Mileage and more for 1948-2018
  • Chemical Substance Registry (CAS registry numbers) - The EPA's Toxic Substances Control Act Chemical Substance Inventory

NASA

Datasets officially released by the United States National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)

  • NASA Open Data Portal - Curated datasets released by NASA
  • Earth Data Search - Earthdata Search is a web application developed by NASA EOSDIS to enable data discovery, search, comparison, visualization, and access across EOSDIS' Earth Science data holdings
  • Landslides After Rainfall, 2007-2016 - Location and cause of landslide events around the world
  • Fireballs - Bolide impacts by the kiloton since 1988
